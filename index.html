⛽Fuel vechile

Fuel vehicles are vehicles that run using petrol or diesel as their source of energy. These vehicles use an internal combustion engine (ICE), where fuel is burned to produce power for movement. Fuel vehicles are widely used because they are easy to refuel and suitable for both personal and commercial transportation Examples: Petrol cars, diesel buses, trucks, motorcycles

⚡Electric vechile

Electric vehicles are vehicles that run using electricity stored in rechargeable batteries. They use an electric motor instead of a fuel engine. Electric vehicles do not produce harmful exhaust gases and are considered environment friendly. Examples: Electric cars, electric scooters, electric buses
